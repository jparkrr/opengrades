OpenGrades

Here are the results of an exam. Remember, the larger the area of a color, the more people who performed in that quartile put that answer.

Step 1:

Find a question you are interested in.

  • The correct answer has a black border.
  • Move your mouse over a question to rearrange by most popular answer.

Step 2:

Each color groups students who got similar grades overall.

  • There are 4 quartiles, green is best.
  • The size of each area tells you how many people in that quartile put that answer.

Step 3:

Look for questions where a lot of people who did well on the test didn't put the right answer.

  • Click the flag if something doesn't seem right.
  • We'll remember your flagged questions.
